Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE) Safety Standards – Stikks

Stikks – Private Social App has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to providing a safe environment for all users.

Our policies include:

  • Immediate removal of any reported content involving CSAE
  • Suspension or banning of accounts involved in CSAE
  • Mandatory reporting to law enforcement authorities for suspected CSAE
  • Age restrictions: Users must be 13 years or older to create an account
  • Moderation and automated detection tools to prevent CSAE content
  • Support for users to report abuse easily via the app or email: support@stikks.app

Stikks complies with all applicable laws regarding CSAE prevention and continually updates our policies to maintain a safe environment.
