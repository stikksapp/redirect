Privacy Policy for Stikks

Effective Date: 01/10/2025

Stikks (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the mobile application Stikks (the “App”) available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Stikks.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and phone number.
  • User Content: The photos, messages, and posts you share with your private groups.
  • Device Information: Basic device data such as model, operating system, and app usage logs.
  • Optional Data: If you grant permission, we may access your camera, microphone, and storage for app features.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the App’s features.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your information. Your content is shared only with the people you choose in your private groups. We may share limited information with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) to keep the app running.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards (encryption, secure storage) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can access and update your account information at any time. You can delete your account, and we will delete your personal data within a reasonable period, unless required by law.

6. Children’s Privacy

Stikks is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new “Effective Date.”
