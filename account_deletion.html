Account Deletion – Stikks : Private Social App

Stikks – Private Social App allows users to permanently delete their account and associated personal data directly from within the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Stikks – Private Social App
  2. Go to the Profile section
  3. Tap the Settings icon in the top-right corner
  4. Click on the Delete Account button
  5. Confirm by clicking on “Delete Account”

Once confirmed, the account deletion process starts immediately.

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile information
  • Posts, messages, and shared content
  • Groups, contacts, and connections
  • Rewards, activity history, and engagement data
  • Any other data associated with the user account

Data retention

All personal user data is permanently deleted after the deletion request. Certain non-personal or legally required records (if applicable) may be retained for up to 90 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance. After this period, all remaining data is permanently erased.
